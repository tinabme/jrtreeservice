Main Services

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Tree trimming and pruning is often seen as an art. With proper management, trees can add value to your home and increase your quality of life. Left unattended, a tree can grow too wild and may eventually die due to being over weighted by debris and/or infested with insects. That’s where periodic maintenance via pruning, trimming, thinning and reduction comes into play.
JR Tree Service has a team skilled at reducing the size of a tree and strategically removing specific branches, leaving it healthier and more beautiful.

Tree Removal

Tree removal may become necessary for a number of reasons.

  • When a tree is diseased, dead, or dying, which can pose a safety risk and more.
  • When necessary as a preventive measure before Hurricane season.
  • When disaster has struck, and the tree or large branches have fallen.
    • JR Tree Service can help you with any of your tree removal needs.

    Stump Grinding & Chipping

    After a tree has been cut or fallen, it's not recommended to leave the stump inplace, as it can be a breeding ground for insects as and drag down the overall appearance of a property. JR Tree Service uses a high power stump grinder, that penetrates deep, chipping and pulverizing the stump and root system.
